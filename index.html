How much does a logo cost?

Logo prices range from UGX 50,000 to over UGX 150,000 depending on the package being delivered to you.

A standard logo pack costs UGX 50,000
A dual logo package that contains different color variants of your logo and mockups costs 100,000
A combo logo package that contains cover designs for socials costs 150,000.

How long does it take to design a logo?

Designing a logo is a process; it can take minutes, hours, weeks, months or even years; time refines an idea and therefore logo designs keep evolving.

However to answer your question the process of shipping a complete logo provided the concept has aleady been drawn can take approximately 48 hours(logo review time inclusive).

Can I get my logo printed on business cards?

You can get your logo printed on any medium that is printable;

We are talking mugs, t-shirts, caps, pens, books, fliers, brochures and more.
